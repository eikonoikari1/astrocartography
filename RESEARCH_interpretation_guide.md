# Part 1: Reading a Natal Chart

## The Delineation Order

Professional astrologers follow a strict priority hierarchy. Do not scan randomly.

1. **Sect** — Day or night chart. Determines which planets are allies vs. adversaries.
2. **Ascendant + chart ruler** — The lens of the whole chart. The chart ruler's sign, house, and aspects = the native's primary life direction.
3. **Sect light** — Sun (day charts) or Moon (night charts). Its house = core area of life focus.
4. **Angular planets** — Planets in houses 1, 4, 7, 10 dominate the life narrative regardless of dignity. Angular > dignified but cadent.
5. **Benefic/malefic of sect** — In a day chart: Jupiter helps most, Mars harms most. Night chart: Venus helps most, Saturn harms most. Their house placements = where life is easiest and hardest.
6. **Dignified and debilitated planets** — Domicile/exaltation = ease. Detriment/fall = struggle that produces unique capability.
7. **Stelliums, Saturn, Nodes** — Additional narrative texture.

**Key principle:** Establish which 3-4 planets matter most before getting lost in 40+ factors.

Sources: [Alice Bell](https://alicebell.substack.com/p/the-first-7-steps-i-take-when-interpreting), [Astrology Club](https://astrologyclub.org/birth-chart-interpretations/synthesis/)

Sect is not about strength — it's about **qualitative expression**. Same planet, same sign, radically different experience depending on sect.

## Planets in Signs: The Guest-Host Metaphor

A planet in a sign is a **guest in the sign ruler's house**. The guest must operate by the host's rules.

- **Mars in Aries (domicile):** Mars at home. Pure, direct, unmodified martial expression.
- **Mars in Virgo (Mercury's sign):** Mars through Mercury's lens — intellectual combat, precise cutting speech, methodical action. Then check Mercury's own condition: if Mercury is strong, Mars in Virgo operates effectively. If Mercury is weak, Mars becomes scattered and anxious.

| Dignity | Metaphor | Effect |
|---------|----------|--------|
| **Domicile** | At home | Effortless, clear, direct |
| **Exaltation** | Honored guest | Enhanced, elevated, almost magical ease |
| **Detriment** | In exile | Must find unconventional expression; struggle produces unique insight |
| **Fall** | At lowest | Greatest challenge; must creatively redirect core nature |

A planet in fall is NOT "bad" — it develops hard-won wisdom. Mars in Cancer (fall) redirects combative energy toward emotional protection.

## Houses: Where Life Plays Out

Signs modify **how** a planet expresses (style). Houses determine **where** (life domain).

- **Venus in the 6th:** Finding pleasure in routines, beauty in mundane work, love as acts of service
- **Venus in the 5th:** Romance is dramatic, creative, playful — Venus in its natural playground
- Same planet, same core nature, completely different life department

**Angular houses** (1, 4, 7, 10) → prominent, powerful, visible
**Succedent houses** (2, 5, 8, 11) → moderate expression
**Cadent houses** (3, 6, 9, 12) → internalized, less visible

## House Rulership Chains: Building the Life Narrative

The ruler of a house is a "roving delegate" — it carries the themes of its home house to wherever it sits.

**Formula:** "The matters of House X are connected to and expressed through House Y."

- **Ruler of 7th in 10th:** Partnerships intertwined with career. Meet partners through work. Relationships are publicly visible.
- **Ruler of 1st in 4th:** Identity built through family, heritage, home.
- **Ruler of 2nd in 7th:** Income comes through partnerships.

**Chain reading:** If ruler of 7th is in 10th, and ruler of 10th is in 3rd → partnerships (7th) → career (10th) → communication/siblings (3rd).

**Derivative houses:** "Partner's money" = 2nd from 7th = 8th house. "Mother's career" = 10th from 4th = 1st house.

The ruling planet's condition matters enormously. Well-dignified ruler in an angular house = ease connecting those life areas. Debilitated ruler in a cadent house = difficulty and indirectness.

## Synthesizing Contradictions

When dignity says one thing and aspects say another:

1. **Flag every contradiction explicitly.** Don't ignore them.
2. **Weigh the testimony:** Angular > cadent. Chart ruler > random planet. Repeated themes > isolated indicators.
3. **Check contextual resolution:** "Shy yet sociable" might mean Moon in Scorpio in 12th (shy privately) + Venus in Libra in 10th (charming publicly). Different houses = different contexts.
4. **Accept irreducible complexity:** Mars conjunct Venus = simultaneously combative and charming. Not a contradiction to resolve — a tension to describe.
5. **Use temporal layers:** Dignity = baseline capacity. Aspects = dynamic events. Well-dignified + harsh aspects = natural talent + external obstacles.

**Master principle:** Do not reduce planetary condition to a single score. Hold multiple factors in tension and read the gestalt.

# Part 2: Aspects — What They Do

## The Five Major Aspects

**Conjunction (0°):** Two energies merge into one. Often a blind spot — the person embodies the blend so completely they can't see it. Quality depends entirely on planets involved. Venus-Jupiter = effortless abundance. Mars-Saturn = grinding frustration as identity.

**Sextile (60°):** Latent talent requiring conscious activation. Doors unlocked but you must push them open. Indicates where skill develops through deliberate practice.

**Square (90°):** The engine of growth through friction. Planets at cross-purposes. "Our greatest life accomplishments can be described by the nature of planets in square." (Frank Clifford). In youth = frustration. With maturity = driving force behind achievement.

**Trine (120°):** Natural, effortless harmony. So natural it can go undeveloped. A chart of all trines and no squares = enormous talent and no drive to use it. Shows what comes naturally, not necessarily what gets used.

**Opposition (180°):** The relational aspect. Awareness through polarity and projection. One end often externalized onto partners/enemies. Developmental task: own both ends.

## Orbs, Applying, and Separating

- **Tight orbs (0-2°):** More unconscious and habitual. "Just who I am."
- **Wide orbs (5-8°):** More conscious and deliberate.
- **Applying aspects** (faster planet moving toward exactness): Stronger, more urgent. Themes become MORE prominent with age.
- **Separating aspects** (past exactness, moving apart): Themes felt MORE in youth, gradually fading.

Standard orbs: Conjunction 8-10°, Opposition/Trine 8°, Square 7-8°, Sextile 5-6°. Luminaries get wider orbs.

## Aspect Patterns

| Pattern | Structure | Meaning |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| **T-Square** | Opposition + both square apex | Relentless tension channeled through apex planet. The workhorse. Cardinal = crisis-driven action. Fixed = stubborn determination. Mutable = mental restlessness. |
| **Grand Trine** | 3 planets all trine, same element | Closed circuit of ease. Risk of inertia/complacency. Talent that goes undeveloped without squares elsewhere. |
| **Grand Cross** | 2 oppositions at right angles + 4 squares | Extreme tension, enormous energy. Rapid crisis succession. Must balance all 4 points simultaneously. |
| **Yod** | 2 sextiles + both quincunx apex | "Finger of God." Fated tension. Solutions through insight, not force. Sense of mission. |
| **Stellium** | 3+ planets in same sign/house | Overwhelming concentration. Extraordinary depth in one domain, potential neglect of everything else. |
| **Kite** | Grand trine + 1 planet opposing one corner | Grand trine given direction and purpose. The opposition provides drive to actually use the talent. |
| **Mystic Rectangle** | 2 oppositions + 2 trines + 2 sextiles | Resolution through release. Problems solve themselves through letting go rather than direct confrontation. |

**T-Square delineation:** Start with apex planet → examine the opposition → look for release aspects (trines/sextiles from pattern planets) → study apex sign/house closely.

## Mutual Reception

Two planets in each other's ruling signs (e.g., Mercury in Aries + Mars in Gemini). Creates a hidden alliance — a "back-channel of energy." Approximates trine quality. Significantly offsets detriment/fall. The houses they occupy become closely connected.

Does NOT require an aspect between them. When they do aspect each other, the aspect colors the cooperation quality.

## Out-of-Sign (Dissociate) Aspects

Planets within orb but in signs that don't share the expected relationship (e.g., trine between fire and earth signs). They ARE real aspects. Use tighter orbs (3-4° max). Hard aspects may be softened, easy aspects complicated. The person experiences the aspect as confusing or inconsistent.

# Part 4: Timing Interpretation

## Transit Interpretation

### What Different Transits Feel Like

**Jupiter conjunct natal Venus:** World is cushioned. Capacity to receive love/money/pleasure expands. Social, romantic, generous. Risk: complacency. Duration: weeks to a couple months.

**Saturn conjunct natal Venus:** Emotional audit. Relationships scrutinized. Question your own worth. Superficial connections crack; solid ones deepen. Avoid major relationship decisions during the harsh initial phase. Duration: 6-9 months with retrogrades.

### Outer vs. Inner Planet Transits

- **Inner planets** (Moon through Mars): Hours to 1-2 months. Daily weather. Minor unless triggering larger configuration.
- **Outer planets** (Jupiter through Pluto): Months to years. Climate change. Saturn = 6-9 months. Pluto = 2-3 years hovering over one point.

### Transits to Planets vs. Angles

- **To natal planets:** Internal/psychological. You may or may not notice externally.
- **To natal angles (ASC, MC, IC, DC):** Inevitable, externally visible events. "We always notice transit conjunctions to our natal angles."

Saturn transiting MC = career will undoubtedly be affected. Mars crossing ASC = visible energy shift others can see.

## Secondary Progressions Interpretation

**Progressed Moon changing signs (~every 2.5 years):** Entire emotional orientation shifts. Cancer = nest and seek security. Aries = restless independence. Capricorn = reserved, achievement-focused.

**Progressed Sun changing signs (~once per 30 years, if at all):** Identity and life orientation shifts. Unmistakable. What interested you for years suddenly fails to hold attention.

**Progressed Venus stationing retrograde:** Turn inward regarding relationships. More reclusive, less interested in socializing. Reassess values. Lasts ~42 years.

**Progressed Venus stationing direct:** "Coming out." Self-image improves. Unhealthy friendships fall away. More sociable. The station year = most dramatic.

## Solar Return Interpretation

### Reading Order
1. **SR Ascendant** — Your temperament for the year. Its ruler = "ruling planet for the year."
2. **SR Moon** — Where your heart is. House = emotional investment area.
3. **Planets on SR angles** — Powerfully define the year. Saturn on SR MC = career pressure. Venus on SR ASC = relationships define experience.

### Overlay onto Natal
Note which natal planets fall on SR angles. Natal Venus on SR ASC = relationships become central. Natal Saturn on SR MC = career obligations intensify.

### Big Year vs. Quiet Year
**Big year indicators:** SR Sun hard-aspects SR Moon. 3+ personal planets angular. Planets at 0° or 29°. Chart ruler angular and well-aspected.
**Quiet year indicators:** Planets mostly cadent. SR Moon in 12th. Few angular placements. No hard Sun-Moon aspects.

## Zodiacal Releasing Interpretation

### What L1 Periods Feel Like

The planetary ruler of each sign colors the experience. **Sect is foundational** — same planet, radically different experience by sect.

- **Mars-ruled (Aries/Scorpio):** Day chart = combative, potentially destructive. Night chart = energizing, action-oriented.
- **Venus-ruled (Taurus/Libra):** Smoother, especially night charts. Pleasure, relationships, creativity.
- **Saturn-ruled (Capricorn/Aquarius):** Demanding, structured, felt as restriction. But long enough for loosing of the bond → deep structural change.
- **Jupiter-ruled (Sagittarius/Pisces):** Expansive, fortunate-feeling. Opportunities, meaning, faith.

### L1 vs. L2
L1 = decades-long life chapters (background climate). L2 = sub-chapters within (foreground weather). L3/L4 = progressively finer timing. Weight decreases at each level.

### Loosing of the Bond
Occurs in signs with periods >17.5 years. The sequence jumps to the opposing sign. **Feels like a major life pivot** — career change, relocation, fundamental directional shift. Schwarzenegger's loosing of the bond: bodybuilding → acting → governorship.

### Angular Triad (Peak Periods)
Signs in the same modality as Fortune's sign are **peak periods** — maximum activity and visibility. But peak ≠ automatically good. If your out-of-sect malefic is activated at peak, it's stressful even as things are externally active.

## The Timing Funnel: Layering Techniques

From broadest to narrowest:

1. **Zodiacal Releasing** → What life chapter? (decades)
2. **Firdaria** → What planet governs this era? (years)
3. **Annual Profections** → Lord of the Year (year)
4. **Solar Return** → Blueprint for birthday-to-birthday (year)
5. **Monthly profections / lunar returns** → (months)
6. **Transits** → The trigger (days/weeks)

### The Rule of Three
When 3+ independent techniques converge on the same planet/sign/theme, prediction confidence is high.

### When Techniques Disagree
Higher-level technique sets context; lower-level provides texture. A hard Saturn transit during a Jupiter profection year will be felt but won't define the year. **Transits are triggers, not causes** — they only produce major events when the planet is already "switched on" by time-lord techniques.

### Synthesis Example
L1 Sagittarius period (Jupiter-ruled, expansion) + profections activate 7th house (Venus as lord) + SR has Venus on MC + transiting Jupiter crosses natal Venus in March → major relationship development with public visibility, triggered around March.

## Planet-Line Experiential Guide

**Venus DS line:** Meet the love of your life. Relationships flow easily, life feels aesthetically pleasing, socially rich. *But* if natal Venus is badly placed, even a Venus line amplifies relationship difficulty with Venusian themes.

## Proximity & Timing

**How close do you need to be?**
- Within 25 miles = intense
- Within 50 miles = strong
- Within 100 miles = noticeable
- 150+ miles = minimal
- 50-150 miles may be the sweet spot — benefit without overwhelm

**How quickly do effects develop?**
- Initial shifts within weeks to months
- Full integration takes 1-4 years
- Leaving a location: effects decompress gradually over months

**Critical principle:** Natal chart condition determines how a line manifests. A benefic line with a natally afflicted benefic ≠ pure blessing. The line amplifies whatever the natal planet carries.

# Part 6: Professional Techniques

## Synastry (Relationship Compatibility)

**Strongest positive indicators (+4 weight):** Sun/Moon conjunct partner's Descendant. Sun/Moon conjunct partner's North Node.

**Very strong (+3):** Sun-Moon cross-chart conjunctions/trines/sextiles (single most important contact). Venus-Mars harmonious aspects (chemistry). Moon-Venus harmonious aspects (nurturing).

**Red flags (-4):** Moon square Saturn (emotional coldness). Mercury square Mercury (can't communicate). Venus square Saturn (affection blocked). Mars square Saturn (resentment buildup).

**House overlays:** Partner's planets in your 5th = romance. In your 7th = partnership. In your 8th = deep/transformative bond. In your 4th = domestic comfort.

## Electional Astrology (Choosing Best Timing)

**The Moon is primary:**
- Never start during void-of-course Moon
- Match Moon sign to goal (Taurus for finance, Leo for creative, Cancer for home)
- Moon's last applying aspect should be harmonious to a benefic
- New Moon for fresh starts, Full Moon for launches/reveals

**Chart ruler:** Place in angular house (1, 4, 7, 10). Avoid 6th, 8th, 12th.
**Retrograde avoidance:** Mercury Rx for contracts, Venus Rx for weddings, Mars Rx for competition.

```bash

node astro.mjs chart

# 2. Get planet strengths (dignity, sect, aspects)
node astro.mjs natal-strength

# 3. Get houses for birth location
node astro.mjs houses '{"lat":56,"lon":92.87}'
```

| **Progressed Lunation** | From `progressions`: Moon lon - Sun lon (mod 360). 0-45°=New, 45-90°=Crescent, etc. |
| **Solar Arc Directions** | From `progressions`: arc = progressed Sun - natal Sun. Add to all natal points. Check for aspects within 1°. |
| **Lots** | From `houses` (Ascendant) + `chart` (planet lons). Fortune = Asc + Moon - Sun (day). |
| **Relocated Chart** | `houses` at new lat/lon + natal planet longitudes → which house does each planet fall in? |
| **Planetary Returns** | `transits` searching for conjunction of planet to its own natal longitude → exact date → `chart` + `houses` at that date. |
| **Cyclocartography** | `lines` at transit date. Compare to `lines` at birth date. Convergences = activated locations. |
