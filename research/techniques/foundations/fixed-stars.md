# Fixed Stars

## Technique Profile

- Practice role: specialized support layer
- Doctrine: traditional / medieval
- Birth-time sensitivity: helpful to required
- Status: planned

## What Question This Answers

Does a tight conjunction to a major fixed star meaningfully intensify a natal planet or angle?

## Use When

- the chart already shows a relevant theme
- there is a tight conjunction to a major star
- you want refinement, not a replacement reading

## Do Not Use When

- there is no tight conjunction
- the star is being treated as a keyword generator
- the rest of the chart does not support the claim

## How to Read It

1. Restrict attention to tight conjunctions.
2. Prioritize angles, luminaries, and highly important planets.
3. Let the natal chart set the topic; let the star refine tone and intensity.

## What Not to Do

- Do not read stars by sign alone.
- Do not use wide orbs.
- Do not let a famous star meaning outrank the natal testimony.

## Current App Support

Planned. This belongs in the app only if it stays narrow, disciplined, and strongly integrated with natal condition.
