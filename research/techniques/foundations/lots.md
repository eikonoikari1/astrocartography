# Lots

## Technique Profile

- Practice role: supporting foundational layer
- Doctrine: traditional / Hellenistic
- Birth-time sensitivity: required
- Status: derivable, with room for expansion

## What Question This Answers

Which derived points reveal specific life topics, especially when timing or topical refinement needs more structure?

## Use When

- you are already working in a traditional framework
- the birth time is solid
- Fortune or Spirit would sharpen an existing reading

## Do Not Use When

- you are mixing traditions carelessly
- the birth time is uncertain
- you want to throw many lots into a reading just to create density

## How to Read It

1. Decide which doctrinal set you are using.
2. Start with Fortune and Spirit before more specialized lots.
3. Read the lot by sign, ruler, house, and current activation.
4. Use additional lots only if they answer a real topical question.

## What Not to Do

- Do not compute many lots without a reason.
- Do not mix formula traditions silently.
- Do not read lots apart from their rulers and activation.

## Current App Support

Fortune logic is already present in the zodiacal releasing implementation. Other lots are derivable from current chart and house outputs but do not yet have dedicated commands.
