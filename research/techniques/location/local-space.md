# Local Space

## Technique Profile

- Practice role: advanced directional location method
- Doctrine: locational astrology
- Birth-time sensitivity: exact required
- Status: planned

## What Question This Answers

In which literal directions from a place do planetary qualities become emphasized?

## Use When

- the question is local and directional
- you want to analyze where within a region, city, or property a line of emphasis may point
- ordinary ACG and relocated work have already framed the larger answer

## Do Not Use When

- you need a global location ranking
- the birth time is weak
- the client question is really about life chapter timing rather than space

## How to Read It

1. Convert planetary positions into horizon-based bearings for the location.
2. Read the relevant planet's natal condition.
3. Use the result as an orientation tool inside a broader location analysis.

## What Not to Do

- Do not treat it as a replacement for global astrocartography.
- Do not over-interpret small directional differences.
- Do not use it without clear place-specific questions.

## Current App Support

Planned. The astronomy is straightforward, but the interpretive framing needs to stay narrow and practical.
