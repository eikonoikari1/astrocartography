# Firdaria

## Technique Profile

- Practice role: supporting chapter layer
- Doctrine: traditional / medieval
- Birth-time sensitivity: required
- Status: planned

## What Question This Answers

Which planet governs this life era, and which sub-period colors the current stretch within it?

## Use When

- you want another broad timing layer between natal promise and annual triggers
- you are working within a traditional framework
- you want to contextualize longer periods by planetary rulership

## Do Not Use When

- you are already overloaded with timing methods
- the birth time or sect determination is weak
- you want event-level prediction from it alone

## How to Read It

1. Determine sect.
2. Identify the major period ruler.
3. Identify the sub-period ruler.
4. Read the natal condition of those rulers and compare them with the current annual activation.

## What Not to Do

- Do not make it the sole predictive engine.
- Do not use it without sect.
- Do not stack it automatically just because it exists.

Planned. The technique is a good candidate for code because it is structurally simple and interpretively useful.
