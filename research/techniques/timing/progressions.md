# Progressions

## Technique Profile

- Practice role: supporting developmental timing layer
- Doctrine: modern / hybrid
- Birth-time sensitivity: helpful to required
- Status: implemented

## What Question This Answers

How is the person changing internally over time?

## Use When

- the question is developmental, psychological, or identity-based
- you want to understand longer inner shifts behind visible events
- a timing stack needs nuance beyond transits alone

## Do Not Use When

- the consultation is strictly event prediction and the natal baseline is still unclear
- the birth time is too uncertain for angle or house-sensitive judgments
- you are using progressions to manufacture certainty where broader timing layers disagree

## How to Read It

1. Start with progressed Sun, Moon, Venus, and any stations.
2. Read sign changes and phase changes before minor aspects.
3. Use progressions to describe internal orientation and life phase.
4. Let transits show when the progressed material externalizes.

## What Not to Do

- Do not read every progressed aspect as a major life event.
- Do not speak as if progressions replace profections or chapter methods.
- Do not ignore natal context when a progression looks dramatic.

## Current App Support

`node astro.mjs progressions`

The app returns progressed planetary longitudes through time. Progressed lunation and solar arc interpretation remain manual or planned.
