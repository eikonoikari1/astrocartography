# Zodiacal Releasing

## Technique Profile

- Practice role: advanced chapter-level timing
- Doctrine: traditional / Hellenistic
- Birth-time sensitivity: exact required
- Status: implemented, partial

## What Question This Answers

Which life chapter is active, and how is it subdivided into major and minor phases?

## Use When

- the birth time is trustworthy
- you need long-form chapter structure
- you want to see whether current life emphasis matches natal topics and current transits

## Do Not Use When

- the birth time is approximate
- you want a quick forecast without doctrinal discipline
- you are mixing it casually with modern interpretive language without naming the blend

## How to Read It

1. Identify the active L1 period and its ruler.
2. Check the active L2 as the current subchapter.
3. Read sect before judging whether a ruler feels supportive or difficult.
4. Check whether the same planet or topic is activated elsewhere in the stack.

## What Not to Do

- Do not use the current implementation as if it were the full traditional technique.
- Do not speak about peak periods or loosing of the bond unless those rules are actually applied.
- Do not use ZR as a standalone forecasting machine.

## Current App Support

`node astro.mjs zodiacal-releasing`

The app currently supports Lot of Fortune releasing with L1-L2 period generation. Spirit releasing, loosing of the bond, and explicit peak-period logic are still planned.
