# Annual Profections

## Technique Profile

- Practice role: core yearly activation method
- Doctrine: traditional / Hellenistic
- Birth-time sensitivity: required
- Status: derivable

## What Question This Answers

Which house and ruler are activated this year?

## Use When

- you need a clean yearly focus
- you want a simple but powerful bridge between natal promise and annual events
- you are building a traditional timing stack

## Do Not Use When

- the house doctrine is undefined
- the birth time is unreliable
- you want profections to replace broader chapter methods

## How to Read It

1. Determine the profected house for the age.
2. Identify the lord of that sign using whole-sign logic.
3. Read the natal condition of the Lord of the Year.
4. Track transits, solar return emphasis, and other annual indicators to that ruler.

## What Not to Do

- Do not read the profected house in isolation from its ruler.
- Do not mix whole-sign profections with quadrant-house assumptions without stating it.
- Do not treat profections as an event list.

## Current App Support

No dedicated command yet. The current CLI gives enough natal and timing data to derive profections manually and integrate them with the broader stack.
