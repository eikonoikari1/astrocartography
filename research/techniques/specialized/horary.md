# Horary

## Technique Profile

- Practice role: separate discipline
- Doctrine: traditional / horary
- Birth-time sensitivity: question-time exactness
- Status: implemented

## What Question This Answers

What is the likely answer to a specific, sincere, present-tense question asked at a known moment?

## Use When

- the consultation is explicitly horary
- the client has one focused question
- the question has real urgency or significance

## Do Not Use When

- you only want another opinion about a natal or relocation reading
- the question is vague or hypothetical
- you are using horary to bypass missing natal data

## How to Read It

1. Establish querent and quesited houses.
2. Judge their rulers, condition, and relevant aspects.
3. Include the Moon as co-significator and witness.
4. Keep the reading inside horary logic.

## What Not to Do

- Do not stack horary into a natal synthesis as if it were just another technique.
- Do not read it without a clear question.
- Do not use it to compensate for weak birth data in a different type of consultation.

## Current App Support

`node astro.mjs horary`

The app can cast the chart, identify rulers, evaluate basic dignity, and inspect aspects and combustion. Full professional horary judgment still requires practitioner interpretation.
